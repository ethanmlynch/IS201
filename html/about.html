About Me

This is who I am!

I was born in Provo Utah to wonderful parents. I am the oldest child in my family and have 4 younger siblings who are the best. I am a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints and my belief in God is what makes me who I am. If you're interested in learning a bit about what I believe, visit churchofjesuschrist.org.

 

I love everyting outdoors. Working hard is my favorite hobby, I always strive to to my best. I love to ski, surf, mountain bike, rock climb, snowboard, ride dirt bikes, wakeboard, and play soccer. Basically if it involes physical activity I'm always down.

I have been blessed to be able to travel the world giving me the fantastic opportunity to expereince many different cultures. I love spending quality time with people and have a special ability to plan and host events that bring people together
